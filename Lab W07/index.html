Welcome to World of Warcraft

World of Warcraft is one of the most successful MMORPGs ever created. Released in 2004 by Blizzard Entertainment, the game takes place in the massive fantasy world of Azeroth.

Players explore continents, complete quests, defeat powerful enemies, and work together in dungeons and raids.

This website explores characters, development history, fan creations, and common questions about World of Warcraft.
