Top Five Characters in World of Warcraft

Arthas Menethil

Arthas is one of the most iconic characters in Warcraft history. His transformation into the Lich King is one of the most famous story arcs in gaming.

Thrall

Thrall is a powerful shaman and former Warchief of the Horde. He represents honor, leadership, and strength.

Jaina Proudmoore

Jaina is one of the most powerful mages in Azeroth. Her story includes betrayal, conflict, and redemption.

Illidan Stormrage

Known as the "Betrayer," Illidan is one of the most complex characters in Warcraft lore.

Sylvanas Windrunner

Sylvanas is a powerful and controversial leader whose decisions shaped major WoW storylines.
