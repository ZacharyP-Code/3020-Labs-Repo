World of Warcraft Q&A

What is World of Warcraft?

World of Warcraft is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game set in the fantasy world of Azeroth.

What are the two main factions?

The two primary factions are the Alliance and the Horde.

What are classes?

Classes determine a character’s abilities and combat style.

What is a raid?

A raid is a large group activity where players work together to defeat powerful bosses.

Why is WoW still popular?

WoW continues to receive expansions and updates.
