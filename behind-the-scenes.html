Behind the Scenes of World of Warcraft

World of Warcraft was developed by Blizzard Entertainment and released in 2004. The development team created a massive online world capable of supporting millions of players across the globe.

The game required years of design, programming, art development, and testing. Developers continue to update the world with expansions, new systems, and balance changes that keep the game evolving.

For this website project, I focused on organizing content clearly and ensuring that all pages are connected through consistent navigation. The site was expanded by adding fan content and Q&A pages while maintaining the original layout.
