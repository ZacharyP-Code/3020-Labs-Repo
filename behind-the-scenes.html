Behind the Scenes of the WoW Economy

World of Warcraft was developed by Blizzard Entertainment and released in 2004. The development team created a massive online world that could support millions of players.

The game required years of design, programming, art development, and testing. Developers continuously update the game with expansions and patches.

For this website project, I focused on organizing content clearly and ensuring that all pages are connected through navigation. I expanded the site for Lab W07 by adding fan content and Q&A pages while keeping all previous pages intact.

One challenge I faced was maintaining consistent navigation across all pages. However, by updating each file carefully, I ensured all links work properly.
