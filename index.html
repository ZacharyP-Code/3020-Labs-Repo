World of Warcraft

World of Warcraft (WoW) is one of the most influential and successful MMORPGs ever created. Released in 2004, it takes place in the fantasy world of Azeroth, where players complete quests, battle powerful enemies, and explore massive continents.

Over the years, WoW has introduced expansions, new classes, new races, and countless storylines. Its deep lore and immersive gameplay have kept millions of players engaged for nearly two decades.

This website highlights some of the most interesting aspects of the game, including favorite characters, behind-the-scenes development, and fan contributions.
