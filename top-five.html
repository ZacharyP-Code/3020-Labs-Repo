Top Five Things About World of Warcraft

1. Arthas Menethil

Arthas is one of the most famous characters in Warcraft history. His transformation into the Lich King represents one of the most dramatic story arcs in gaming.

2. Thrall

Thrall is a powerful shaman and former Warchief of the Horde. He symbolizes leadership, honor, and strength.

3. Jaina Proudmoore

Jaina is one of the most powerful mages in Azeroth. Her story involves political conflict, loss, and redemption.

4. Illidan Stormrage

Illidan is known as the "Betrayer." His morally complex character makes him one of the most compelling anti-heroes in the game.

5. Sylvanas Windrunner

Sylvanas is a controversial and powerful leader. Her actions have shaped major storylines in recent expansions.
